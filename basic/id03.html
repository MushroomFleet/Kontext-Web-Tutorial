Basic #03

Wall Material Change

Learn architectural visualization fundamentals through simple building material transformation. This technique showcases FLUX Kontext's understanding of structural elements and material properties in architectural contexts.

Technique Overview

Material transformation in architectural contexts demonstrates FLUX Kontext's sophisticated understanding of building materials and their visual properties. This technique is essential for architects, designers, and real estate professionals who need to visualize different material options:

  • • Material recognition - Intelligent identification of wall surfaces and architectural elements
  • • Texture application - Realistic brick texture with proper scale, mortar lines, and weathering
  • • Lighting adaptation - Shadows and highlights adjusted to match brick surface properties
  • • Contextual preservation - Windows, doors, trim, and other elements remain unchanged

This technique is invaluable for renovation planning, architectural presentations, and helping clients visualize different exterior finish options before construction begins.

FLUX Kontext Prompt

Material Transformation
Make the walls brick

Why This Prompt Works

This concise prompt leverages FLUX Kontext's architectural understanding. "Make the walls" clearly identifies the structural elements to modify, while "brick" provides specific material guidance. The model automatically applies realistic brick textures, patterns, and material properties while preserving all architectural details like windows, doors, and decorative elements.
