Intermediate #28

Gothic Text Addition

Learn to add stylized text with specific font characteristics and visual effects that seamlessly integrate with historical or themed imagery, preserving authentic typography and aging effects.

Technique Overview

Stylized text addition requires understanding both typography and material integration to create believable text that appears naturally inscribed or written on surfaces. This technique demonstrates FLUX Kontext's sophisticated text rendering capabilities:

  • • Typography matching - Accurate recreation of specific historical and artistic font styles
  • • Material integration - Text appears naturally applied to the surface with appropriate texture and aging
  • • Color and ink effects - Realistic ink properties including transparency, saturation, and wear patterns
  • • Positioning precision - Text placement that respects the underlying surface geometry and perspective

This technique is essential for creating period-appropriate documents, fantasy artwork, historical recreations, and themed designs where authentic typography is crucial for maintaining immersion and authenticity.

FLUX Kontext Prompt

Typography Addition Prompt
Add the text 'Secret Map' in an old English gothic script font, dark sepia ink, centered at the top of the scroll.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt succeeds by specifying the exact text content ("Secret Map"), precise typography style ("old English gothic script font"), specific ink properties ("dark sepia ink"), and exact positioning ("centered at the top of the scroll"). This level of detail ensures consistent and authentic results.
